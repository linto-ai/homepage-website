How does it work ?

1 2 3 4
1/ Voice enabled client
  • Wake up LinTO client with custom HotWords
  • Meeting mode with spontaneous multi-speaker speech
  • Simple voice commands or larger vocabulary
2/ On premise or SaaS Speech to text API
  • Flexible and scalable API to handle amounts of streams & audio files
  • AI based transcription that uses your specific vocabulary
  • State of the art WER
3/ Evaluation
  • AI based Natural Language Understanding engine
  • Textual definition of suitable intentions and easily map the corresponding skills
  • Automated data augmentation for lexicals (dictionaries and scenarios)
4/ Trigger anything with LinTO
  • Modular skills to access any devices, APIs, productivity platforms etc.
  • Integrates securely into Complex workflows
  • Graphically describe and parametrize your use-cases

Use cases

At the heart of your meeting room

  • Topology evaluation using cameras and audio beamforming (spatial filtering)
  • Large vocabulary speech to text for multiple speakers spontaneous speech
  • Display live contextual recommendations
  • Helps you deal with meeting minutes with AI driven summarizations

Hands-free processes for better productivity

  • Voice access to your technical datasheets, databases, anything !
  • Vocal or visual feedback on any display
  • Expand capabilities with technical and specific vocabulary

Vocal control anywhere in your office

  • Smoothly operate a fleet of voice access end-points
  • Vocal access to Chatbots, smart-building, facility management and more
  • Quickly mockup, develop and deploy specific skills for custom workflows

Voice access for end-user applications

  • Add voice control to your B2C software
  • Consume Speech To Text API in your existing Mobile or Web application
  • Data stays private for HealthCare, banking or any super-secure use-cases

The Open Source framework that propels Business-aware, private by design, Smart-Assistants

  • Use the LinTO stack in your own Smart-Speaker / Smart-Assistant hardware design
  • Build a custom smart-assistant without any external reference to Alexa or Google Home
  • Monitor and operates a fleet of LinTO clients deployed anywhere
