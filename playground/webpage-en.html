How does it work ?

Welcome to the demonstration page of LinTO in a web browser. Navigation and actions in a browser can be triggered by your voice thanks to the LinTO assistant. Please follow the instructions to use vocal commands.

1/ Allow the microhpone access to the webpage

Once the webpage is loaded, a notification should pop in your browser, asking you to allow microphone access.

Please allow the use of your microphone.

2/ Say the wake word

Once you've allowed the use of your microphone, you should see LinTO appear on the top left corner of your browser. When he is "asleep" you can wake him up by saying the wake word clearly, and then he will be ready to listen to your commands.

Say : "LinTO"

If LinTO correctly understood the wake-up-word, you should hear a "beep" and see LinTO changing waking up. He is now listening!

3/ Say a command

Once LinTO is "listening", try using one of the commands displayed on the webpage.

Say : "go to next block"
