Try LinTO in your web browser and navigate with your voice

How to use LinTO on your web browser

You should have a notification form your web browser to enable audio capture from your computre.
Please accept it for being able to use LinTO.

Once you have enabled the audio capture, you should see LinTO on the top left corner of the webpage.
He is asleep, isn't he ?

> To wake LinTO up, you have to say :

"LinTO"

Now, LinTO should be awaken, and he is waiting for you to ask him something.
Begin by asking LinTO to go to the next section.

> To go to the next section, you have to say :

"Please go to the next section"
